Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I book a bus ticket?

To book a bus ticket, simply visit the booking page, select your destination, date, and seat, then proceed to payment.

Can I cancel my ticket?

Yes, tickets can be canceled from the "My Bookings" page. Cancellation charges may apply as per the policy.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, UPI, mobile wallets, and net banking.

How do I track my bus?

You can track your bus in real-time by logging in to your account and clicking on "Track Bus" in the menu.

Are refunds available for canceled tickets?

Refunds are available as per our cancellation policy. The refund amount will be credited to your account within 5-7 working days.

Is it safe to book tickets online?

Yes, our platform uses secure payment gateways to ensure the safety and security of your transactions.
